Bhagavad Gita in Brief

Krishna teaches Arjuna that right action must arise from inner steadiness, not fear, ego, or attachment to results. When the mind becomes clear, duty turns into worship.

The Gita unites karma, jnana, dhyana, and bhakti. It does not ask us to run away from life; it asks us to transform the way we live it.
